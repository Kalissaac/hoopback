About

What is Hoopback?

Hoopback is a service that acts a webhook to webhook middleware, taking the contents of one webhook and sending it to another. This can be useful when the formats between two different webhooks are incompatible, such as with Discord or Slack webhooks.

Easy to Use

Simply set up your destination webhook, what fields to port over and give it a name and Hoopback will do the rest! If you have any special requirements, there is also the ability to create more advanced transformations.

Status Updates

Get the latest status updates from a platform sent directly to your Discord or Slack channel! It's never been easier to keep tabs on all of your services. Hoopback allows you to manage all of your webhooks in one place, and you have total control over your webhook.
